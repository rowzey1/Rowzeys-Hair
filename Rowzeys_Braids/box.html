Box Braids

Parent to knotless braids, box braids are a popular and iconic protective hairstyle where the hair is sectioned into square parts and braided down the length of each section.

Here are some key features of box braids:

  1. Protective Style: Box braids are a low-maintenance, protective style that tucks the natural hair away, reducing exposure to environmental damage and minimizing daily manipulation.
  2. Low Maintenance: Once installed, box braids require minimal styling and maintenance. Regularly moisturizing the scalp and using lightweight oils helps keep the hair and scalp healthy while reducing frizz.
  3. Versatility: They can be styled in various sizes and lengths, from small and thin to large and thick, depending on preference.
  4. Long-Lasting: Box braids can last anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks with proper maintenance, making them an ideal choice for those seeking a long-lasting, protective style. Regular upkeep of the scalp and edges helps extend the life of the braids.
  5. There are a Variety of Add-on's that can be included with knotless braids. Here are some options I offer:
    • Boho: Addition of curly hair extensions to achieve a carefree, bohemian, or beachy look.
    • Hot water curls: Use of curl rods and hot water to create curls.
    • Beads: Addition of beads, and securing them by using rubber bands or buring the ends

In summary, box braids are a stylish, protective hairstyle that offers versatility, longevity, and the opportunity for creative expression. Whether worn with or without extensions, they provide a low-maintenance solution for those looking to protect and grow their natural hair.
