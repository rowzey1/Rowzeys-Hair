Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do i book an appointment?
    • Message me on Instagram with a reference picture of the style you are interested in.
    • Fill out the "Get in touch" form below.
  2. Who provides the hair?
    • I do not provide hair, but im happy to give recomendations on what type of hair to buy, and how many packs we may use.
  3. How should I prep my hair?
    • Come with hair washed.
    • Preferably, hair shouldbe blowdried or stretched out for styles such as cornrows, knotless braids, etc. This allows for easier manipulation of the hair.
    • Hair should be free of product, and oils.
  4. When do i get your address?
    • For new clients, I send out my address 24 hours prior to the appointmnet time with instructions about parking.
  5. What is the cost of service/travel?
    • Pricing is based on a variety of factors i.e parts size, length, fullness of hair, travel distance etc. A quote will be given after a discussion of your vision, and/or examination of the reference picture you sent.
